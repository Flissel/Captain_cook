# Company: TechStartup GmbH

## Industry
B2B SaaS — Developer Productivity Tools

## Size
50 employees, 15 in Sales/Marketing, 10 in Engineering, 5 in Customer Success

## Goals
- Scale outbound sales pipeline from 100 to 500 qualified leads per month
- Reduce manual CRM data entry by 80% through automation
- Build a content engine that produces 20 blog posts and 5 case studies per quarter
- Achieve 95% customer onboarding completion rate within 30 days

## Departments Needed
- Sales Core (leadership, strategy, pipeline management)
- Outreach (BDR team, email campaigns, social selling, multilingual)
- Lead Qualification (scoring, CRM updates, hand-off to AEs)
- Competitive Intelligence (market research, competitor tracking, product analysis)
- Content & Enablement (blog posts, case studies, sales collateral, presentations)
- Revenue Operations (reporting, data warehouse, analytics dashboards)
- Customer Success (onboarding, retention, upsell identification)

## Existing Tools
- HubSpot CRM (primary CRM, deal tracking)
- Slack (internal comms)
- Google Workspace (docs, sheets, slides)
- Stripe (billing)
- Notion (internal wiki)
- GitHub (engineering)

## Budget Constraints
- Lean team — automate repetitive tasks wherever possible
- Prefer open-source or API-based tools over expensive SaaS licenses
- Each agent team should be self-contained and deployable independently
